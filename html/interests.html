Travel

While in Italy I got to take a gondola ride in Venice, walk inside the dome of the Duomo in Florence, and hike along the Cinqua Terre in Liguria. In France we got to go up the Eiffel Tower. My favorite part of the trip was the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, Spain. I've also been to Edmonton and Calgary for various volleyball tournaments.
